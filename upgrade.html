Upgrade

Hey there! If you like the bot and want to show support with a small donation, you can!

Or, if you're interested in some upgraded features, those exist too!

What does upgrading do?

Supporting the bot on Patreon grants you upgrade tokens, which you apply to Discord servers with the /upgrade command. An upgraded server gets the following features:
  • No command cooldowns
  • Allows full customization of button roles
  • Enables the server link feature
    * Only a single server needs to be upgraded, you can link 4 servers together with 1 upgrade token

Additionally, any supporters (not just Patreon) get a fancy role and a private category in the Jot Discord server!

Where do I sign up?

At this time, only Patreon members will receive upgrade tokens. You can become a patron with the button below!
If your Discord account is linked with Patreon, the bot should automatically recognize you within 5 minutes of a successful pledge.
Otherwise, DM the Jot on Patreon with your Discord ID, or DM an administrator in the Jot Discord server to get your tokens. Note that this requires human interaction and can take way longer!
Become a Patron!
Here is how the system works:
  • Upgrade tokens are entirely automatic inside the bot! If you've linked your Discord account in Patreon, all you do is become a patron and run /upgrade to apply one to your server. You can freely move tokens between servers as many times and as often as you want.
  • The Patreon tier you pick will say how many tokens it gives you. The prices and token amounts will never change without at least a full month of warning.
  • When Patreon charges you, the token(s) you are granted will last 32 days. However, when you first become a patron you are granted bonus time equal to how late in the month it is: If you become a patron on the 31st then get charged again the next day (the 1st), your tokens will last 64 days because you didn't get those first 32 days. Those 32 extra days will not be consumed until after you've canceled your pledge!

What happens if I stop being a patron?

When your tokens expire (detailed in the panel above):
  • Command cooldowns come back
  • Existing button roles will remain fully functional, you just can't fully customize any new ones you create
  • Servers will stay linked in the configuration, the links will simply not function until one of the linked servers becomes upgraded again
